Game Overview

In this captivating strategy game, you will play as a tank commander, merging identical tanks to upgrade into a more advanced tank. Each tank possesses unique skills and combat power. You’ll need to employ flexible strategies on the battlefield, directing your tank army to defeat the enemy.

Game Features

Merge to Upgrade: By merging two of the same tanks, you will obtain a higher-level, more powerful tank. Different tank combinations will produce unexpected effects, securing your unbeatable position on the battlefield.

Strategic Battles: In this strategy-packed game, you need to flexibly apply various tactics to counter different enemies. In intense battles, making the right decisions will be the key to victory.

Generous Rewards: Complete game tasks and challenges to win substantial rewards and achievements. Continuously enhance your tank army and become a legendary commander on the battlefield.
