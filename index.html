merge tank shooter

Bingjue123 published on 2023-12-21
Explore the world of merging tanks and embark on a new era of strategic battles! Game Overview In this captivating strategy game, you will play as a tank commander, merging identical tanks to upgrade into a more advanced tank. Each tank possesses unique skills and combat power. You’ll need to employ flexible strategies on the battlefield, directing your tank army to defeat the enemy. Game Features Merge to Upgrade: By merging two of the same tanks, you will obtain a higher-level, more powerful tank.
